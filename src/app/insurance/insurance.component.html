What Happens in Case of Fines or Incidents?

In case of fines or incidents during the rental period, the following procedures apply:

  • Any fines incurred during the rental period are the responsibility of the renter.
  • If the vehicle is damaged due to the renter's negligence or fault, the renter is liable for the repair costs.
  • Insurance coverage may be void in case of reckless driving, driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs, or other violations of the rental agreement.
  • Any damages or fines will be deducted from the security deposit or charged to the renter's credit card.
