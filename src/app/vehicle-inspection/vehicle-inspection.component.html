Vehicle Inspection

Thorough Inspection

Each vehicle undergoes a comprehensive inspection before and after each rental.

Condition Reporting

Detailed condition reports are provided to document the condition of the vehicle.

Conditions Reporting

In case of damage to the rental vehicle, the following procedures apply:

  • The renter must report any damage to the rental agency immediately.
  • The rental agency will assess the damage and determine the necessary repairs.
  • The renter may be liable for the cost of repairs depending on the terms of the rental agreement and insurance coverage.
